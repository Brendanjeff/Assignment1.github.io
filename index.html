Where did my interest in I.T come from?

My interest in IT was sparked when I my father came home one day with our first ever family computer.
I remember sitting next to him, glued to my chair watching him do the simplest of tasks, like typing, and being engrossed by it
because of how fast it was compared to writing. It wasn’t until I watched him take the covers off and replace the HDD that I was
truly hooked on computers, most notably the building side of it all, and have been building my own PC’s since then.

I chose to study with RMIT as it was recommended to me by number of people, and I needed a 100% online option to be able to fit study
in with everything else going on in life.

During my studies, I would expect to learn a broad range of skills that would see me as educated/qualified enough to successfully apply
for most generalised I.T roles in the workplace. I am particularly interested in the cloud based computing and networking components of the course.
